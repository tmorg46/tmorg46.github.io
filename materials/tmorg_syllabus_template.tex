\begin{center} In this class, we follow my elementary school motto: \\ \textbf{``RESPECTFUL, RESPONSIBLE, SAFE''} \end{center}
\bigbreak

\textbf{Respectful:} I get you out of class on time, you wait to pack up until class is over. I respond to emails within a business day, you don't get mad at me when I take longer than 20 minutes to respond. We are all courteous and kind to each other.

\textbf{Responsible:} I come to class prepared to teach, you come to class prepared to learn. I get grades back to you quickly, you turn the assignments in on time. We hold ourselves accountable to each other.

\textbf{Safe:} I keep an open line of communication, you use it to talk to me if situations get difficult. I maintain professional boundaries, you maintain your physical, mental, and emotional health. We build an environment where everyone learns without fear.
