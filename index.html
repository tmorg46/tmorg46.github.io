Hello!

I graduated from BYU with a BS of Economics in April 2023. I work as a pre-doc research assistant in the Record Linking Lab, where I lead the Census Tree Team.

My current research focuses on using historical records to answer economic questions. My main project at the moment is joint authorship with Ariadna Jou on a paper about the effects of New Deal-era relief spending on longevity.
